About Barrett's Quarry

A Family Tradition in Stone

Located in the heart of Bangor, County Mayo, Barrett's Quarry is a proud family-run business dedicated to providing the finest white quartzite stone in the West of Ireland.

Our beautiful white quartzite is renowned for its exceptional quality, durability, and aesthetic appeal. We've built our reputation on reliability, fair pricing, and a commitment to serving both commercial and domestic customers with the same level of care and attention.
